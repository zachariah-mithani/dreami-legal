Common Questions

How do I cancel my subscription?
Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. Go to Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions to manage or cancel your plan.

How does the Dream Visualizer work?
After logging a dream, tap "Visualize" on the dream details page. Dreami will generate a unique AI artwork based on your dream. Free accounts get 1 visualization per month — upgrade to Premium for unlimited.

What are Continuations?
Continuations let you explore where your dream could go next using AI. Free accounts get 1 continuation per month. Premium unlocks unlimited continuations.

What are AI Analyses?
After logging a dream, tap "Analyze" to receive a psychological breakdown of your dream's themes and emotions. Free accounts get 2 analyses per month. Premium unlocks unlimited.

What is Dream Universe?
Dream Universe is an interactive constellation map of all your dreams, showing patterns and connections across your journal over time.

I'm having a technical issue — what should I do?
Try closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, email us at zachmithani@gmail.com with a description of the problem and your device model.
